Embark on a maze mastery adventure at Maze Mechathon! Design, build, and program your robot to navigate intricate mazes, overcome obstacles, and emerge triumphant. Robo Quiz

The RoboQuiz is an engaging and competitive event where participants test their knowledge and expertise in the field of robotics. It typically consists of a series of questions covering various topics such as robotics history, principles, components, applications, and recent advancements. Participants may compete individually or in teams. Overall, the RoboQuiz offers an exciting opportunity for robotics enthusiasts to showcase their knowledge and passion for the field in a fun and competitive environment.
